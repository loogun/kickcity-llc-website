# KickCity LLC — Official Website

A professional business website for KickCity LLC, a Dallas-Fort Worth-based holding company for three youth sports brands serving families across Collin County, North Texas.

## Live Site
[View Live Site](https://kick-city--MideRichard.replit.app)

## About This Project
This website was designed and built using AI-assisted development and self-directed technical learning. It serves as both a functional business website and a portfolio project 
## About KickCity LLC
KickCity LLC is the parent holding company for three youth sports brands focused on youth athletes ages 2–13 (Pre-K through 8th grade) in the McKinney, Melissa, Prosper, Celina, and Anna corridors of Collin County, North Texas.

### Ventures
- **Kick City Soccer Academy** — A youth soccer coaching brand developing foundational skills, teamwork, and sportsmanship in young athletes
- **KickCity Athletics** — A youth sports training brand focused on athletic agility, self-discipline, and resilient mindset development
- **Kick City Soccer House** — A proposed premier indoor and outdoor soccer coaching and play facility serving families across North Texas

## Pages
- **Home** — Company overview, venture previews, and call to action
- **About** — Mission, values, company story, and geography served
- **Ventures** — Detailed overview of all three brands under KickCity LLC
- **Contact** — Inquiry form and location information
